About

Xiangshan county is a county under the jurisdiction of ningbo city, zhejiang province, China. It is located on the coast of the east China sea, at the south edge of the Yangtze river delta region and the east coast of zhejiang province. It is located between xiangshan port and sanmen bay. Tang shenlong two years (AD 706) county, because of the northwest county hill "shape like fu xiang", the name xiangshan. It is composed of 608 islands and reefs in the east of xiangshan peninsula and the coast, covering a land area of 1,175 square kilometers and an ocean area of 5,350 square kilometers. The coastline is 800 kilometers long, accounting for one sixth of the coastline of zhejiang province. It has 10 towns, 5 townships and 3 streets, with a registered population of 503,300 in xiangshan. [1] in 2018, it was selected as a national demonstration zone for quality and safety of export knitted garments.
